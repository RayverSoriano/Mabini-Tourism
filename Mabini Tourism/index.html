About Mabini

Mabini is a beautiful coastal town located in the province of Batangas, Philippines. Known as the "Dive Capital of the Philippines," Mabini offers stunning dive sites, pristine beaches, and breathtaking views of the Taal Volcano. Whether you're an adventure seeker or someone looking for a peaceful getaway, Mabini has something for everyone.

Top Attractions

Anilao Dive Sites

Explore vibrant coral reefs and diverse marine life in one of Asia's top diving destinations.

Mt. Gulugod Baboy

A scenic hiking spot, famous for its pig-backbone-shaped ridge and stunning views of Anilao and Taal Volcano.

Mabini Beaches

Relax on beautiful white sand beaches and enjoy the tranquil seaside atmosphere.

Local Food Favorites

Adobo

A classic Filipino dish with savory marinated meat, perfect for any meal.

Lomi

A Filipino thick noodle soup with meat, veggies, and a savory broth.

Lechon

Whole roasted pig with crispy skin, a festive and flavorful Filipino favorite.
